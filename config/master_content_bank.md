### Long Company Description (120-170 words)
Karim & Associates Limited is a multidimensional professional firm founded in 2014 and led by experienced practitioners in accounting, finance, audit, taxation, management, and organizational development. Over the years, the firm has delivered training, consulting, and advisory support to clients across both public and private sectors in Nigeria and selected international locations.

Our approach combines technical depth with practical implementation. We design customized programs after assessing each client's unique needs, then deploy interactive training methods, applied workshops, and post-training support to ensure measurable impact. Alongside our human capital focus, the firm maintains strong capabilities in statutory audit, assurance, financial reporting, tax management, and governance-focused services.

Karim & Associates is known for quality delivery, professionalism, and a commitment to helping institutions and individuals achieve sustainable improvement in competence, compliance, and organizational performance.
